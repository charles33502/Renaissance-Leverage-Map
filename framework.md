# Renaissance Leverage Map (RLM)
# 文藝復興槓桿地圖

## 1. Definition / 定義

**Renaissance Leverage Map** is a four-column framework for identifying leverage points in a technological turning point.

It focuses on a specific pattern:

> A classical capability was already valuable, but its execution cost was too high.  
> A new technology, such as AI, lowers that cost.  
> As a result, the classical capability becomes newly scalable and strategically important.

**文藝復興槓桿地圖**是一個用於技術拐點時代的四欄分析框架。

它關注的是以下模式：

> 某個古典能力本來就有價值，但過去執行成本太高。  
> 新技術，例如 AI，降低了這個執行成本。  
> 因此，這個古典能力重新變得可規模化，並具有新的策略價值。

---

## 2. Why "Renaissance"? / 為什麼叫文藝復興？

The word **Renaissance** is used because the framework is not centered on novelty for its own sake.

The point is not that AI invents all important abilities from scratch.  
Rather, AI often revives older, classical, and fundamental abilities by making them easier to execute repeatedly and at scale.

「文藝復興」這個詞的意思，不是單純追求新潮工具。

重點不是 AI 從零發明所有重要能力。  
相反地，AI 經常是降低執行成本，使得許多舊有的、古典的、基本的能力重新被啟用、放大與規模化。

---

## 3. Why "Leverage"? / 為什麼叫槓桿？

A leverage point is a place where a small change in cost, speed, or accessibility produces a large change in practical value.

AI creates leverage not only by generating content, but also by reducing friction in:

- searching
- summarizing
- structuring
- translating
- simulating
- reviewing
- debugging
- visualizing
- planning
- documenting
- iterating

槓桿點指的是：成本、速度或可及性的小變化，會造成實務價值的大幅變化。

AI 產生槓桿的方式，不只是生成內容，也包括降低以下摩擦：

- 搜尋
- 摘要
- 結構化
- 翻譯
- 模擬
- 回饋
- 除錯
- 視覺化
- 規劃
- 文件化
- 迭代

---

## 4. Why "Map"? / 為什麼叫地圖？

This framework is a map rather than a single answer.

It is designed to help individuals or teams navigate capability investment under technological change.

It does not ask only:

> What can AI do?

Instead, it asks:

> What becomes newly worth doing because AI lowers the cost?

這個框架比較像地圖，而不是單一答案。

它的用途是幫助個人或團隊在技術變動時，判斷能力投資方向。

它不只是問：

> AI 可以做什麼？

而是問：

> 因為 AI 降低了成本，哪些事情現在重新值得做？

## 6. Core Questions / 核心四問

1. **What is being revived?**  
   Which classical capability, method, theory, or discipline becomes more valuable again?

2. **What cost has AI reduced?**  
   Is it search cost, organization cost, expression cost, feedback cost, verification cost, planning cost, or iteration cost?

3. **What is the durable core?**  
   After removing the tool hype, what human judgment, skill, or theoretical foundation remains important?

4. **Where does it land?**  
   How does this capability matter in learning, research, engineering, career development, product design, or organizational work?

---

1. **復興的是什麼？**  
   哪個古典能力、方法、理論或學科重新變得重要？

2. **AI 降低了什麼成本？**  
   是搜尋成本、整理成本、表達成本、回饋成本、驗證成本、規劃成本，還是迭代成本？

3. **不變的核心是什麼？**  
   拿掉工具熱潮後，真正仍然重要的人類判斷、技能或理論基礎是什麼？

4. **落到哪裡？**  
   這個能力如何落到學習、研究、工程、職涯、產品或組織流程中？

## 8. Relationship to SWOT / 與 SWOT 的差異

SWOT is useful for static strategic analysis:

- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Opportunities
- Threats

Renaissance Leverage Map is more suitable for technological turning points, because it focuses on how cost structures change.

SWOT asks:

> What is my current position?

Renaissance Leverage Map asks:

> What becomes newly valuable because the cost structure has changed?

SWOT 適合穩定時期的現況盤點：

- 優勢
- 劣勢
- 機會
- 威脅

文藝復興槓桿地圖更適合技術拐點時期，因為它關注的是成本結構如何改變。

SWOT 問的是：

> 我現在的位置如何？

文藝復興槓桿地圖問的是：

> 因為成本結構改變，什麼東西重新變得有價值？

## 9. Possible Application: Dynamic Potential Evaluation / 可能應用方向：動態潛力評估

RLM may also be applied as a dynamic potential evaluation tool in future versions.

In this extended use case, instead of asking only whether a person, skill, project, or strategy is strong now, RLM asks:

> Which underlying capabilities may become more valuable under a changing environment?

This is not the primary focus of v0.2.0, but it is included as a possible application direction.

RLM 未來也可以被應用為一種動態潛力評估工具。

在這個延伸用法中，它不只問一個人、一項技能、一個專案或一個策略現在強不強，而是問：

> 哪些底層能力，可能會在環境變動下變得更有價值？

這不是 v0.2.0 的主要功能，而是作為可能應用方向先行記錄。
